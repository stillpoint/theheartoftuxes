    Masturbation (help)

    (Part 1)

    This is a huge topic, and not particularly the domain of Buddhism. Other subreddit and resources might be more appropriate for you.

    In terms of the Buddhist path, the best way (if we understand this as a generalization) is to do it by balancing one's life, helping the mind and energy to settle naturally, and to focus on positive activities rather than try to eradicate a very primal and powerful urge -- and an activity that connects to one's relationship with oneself and the physical and emotional love one may express in intimate relationships with others.

    Especially in the case of young people, and very especially in the case of young men, the sexual urge tends to be very strong, prominent, and persistent. If you try to do away with it you will likely end up fighting with yourself, and losing.

    This is a very, very good and important teaching situation for you and for the rest of us. It highlights the kind of mistaken approach that so often is taken, wherein one tries to get rid of the troublesome part of one's own being. We experience a difficulty or an aspect of our character that we find inconvenient or shameful, and quite naturally we figure that it should go away -- that we'd be better off if it did not exist.

    This is a big, big mistake in spirituality, in religion, and in life. It is a very common but very big mistake.

    In trying to do away with the problem, we turn our backs on an essential part of who we are. This turns into denial, hiding, splitting between public and private faces, and self harm because we disown -- rather than love -- part of who we are.

    The name for this is Spiritual Bypassing. I've written about it several times in previous posts. We have to be careful not to push parts of us into the shadows; because when we do, the control is from there. Instead of having a conscious relationship with the difficult aspect, we turn away from it and it becomes a demon for us.

    If instead we encounter the issue with consciousness and love, that same force in our life becomes awakened energy. Our embrace of our complete humanity allows us to wake up to, and offer compassion to, all others. So it's best to avoid trying to cut off or suppress strong urges and habits.

    Two major initiatives can bring motivation into a more organic and balanced place in your life, reducing the polarity, the separation and distance between real life on one hand and spirituality on the other: balancing the basic structures of life and focusing on positive thought and activity.

    Balancing the fundamental structures of life helps you settle the fiery sexual energy. It doesn't eliminate it, but it helps bring it to a less erratic, flaring state. [These are not necessarily Buddhist teachings, but are drawn from various sources, primarily Taoist and Hindu Yoga and Traditional Chinese Medicine.]

    This balancing includes...

    • Eat moderately rather than stuffing yourself, as overeating promotes flaring sexual energy and other sensual craving

    • Avoid eating before bed

    • Eat more vegetables and less meat, dairy, sugar, and processed foods

    • Eat regularly

    • Eat in a calm and joyful environment and mindset

    • Avoid sleeping too much, as this in particular tends to cause sexual energy and fixation to flare up

    • For best physical and energetic balance go to bed by 10:30pm or earlier, and wake up before dawn

    • Not getting enough sleep harms the body, but too much sleep is worse as it harms the spirit

    • Be vigorously active regularly: at least an hour of activity in which you break a sweat should be enjoyed pretty much daily

    • Include many moments of calmness in your day, either remembering to relax and take a peaceful breath regularly, or setting a timer to ring every fifteen minutes, at which time you should completely let go of everything for a few seconds and let body and mind settle with a peaceful breath

    • Also invite conscious rest (not vegging out) and restful movements and activities into your life, as this and the above point nourish the Yin energy that calms and stabilizes the flaring Yang sexual energy:

    • a simple walk

    • restorative yoga
    • a simple taiji movement (like cloud hands)
    • enjoying a glass of water on the porch
    • listening to birds
    • watching sky and clouds
    • pleasant, calm, Dharmic talk with a friend
    • unstructured stretching on the ground
    • feeling the morning dew with bare feet
    • eating one thing mindfully
    • rocking in a hammock or rocking chair

    • and other calm, Yin activities

    • Keep the body clean

    • Dress in a comfortable and reasonably dignified way

    • Help physical integrity and grounding, whole-body awareness by lightly brushing the entire body with the hands for a few minutes (this is especially useful in cases of obsession, fixation, and addiction)

    • Keep your living space clean and orderly

    • Enjoy possessions that bring you joy; say goodbye to possessions that don't bring you joy

    • Put honored things (like sacred images) in honored places, and arrange your space so you will view clean, noble, and natural things when you look around, rather than messy, low-minded, trivial, or soulless things

    • Strive for balance among work, recreation/creativity, and rest

    • Establish the foundation of a meditation practice by doing very short, enjoyable meditation once or twice a day (rather than forcing and then dropping out); also light a candle or incense or offer a fresh cup of clean water

    • Gradually arrange your relationships so you associate with stable, kind, wise, truth-seeking people, and limit contact with unstable, selfish, confused, small-minded people

    • Whenever possible, meet and spend time with wise teachers and awakened beings

    These are the kinds of life-balancing guidelines that help your energy to stabilize and settle naturally. In Qigong and Taoist internal energy arts these balancing changes are said to sedate the hot fire and erratic wind energies, and grow the cool flowing water energy. It helps sex and sex energy to find a natural connection with the rest of your life.
