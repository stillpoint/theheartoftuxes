    Meditating on compassion?

    Just follow the course and try. You are already tapping into compassion by trying the course and by following up here. Compassion is your original nature, so you don't need to get it elsewhere. Just continue. If you continue practicing, then over time, the experience of returning to your kind and open heart will become more direct and clear and decisive.

    But it is very important not to conceive of compassion as something special, not to think of it as anything in particular. It is not the same thing as kind feelings, as beneficial as they may be. Compassion is open readiness. Look at the images of Kuan Yin in the posture of Royal Ease: at rest, but ready to respond; one foot down as if already taking a step toward those in need. Kuan Yin, the perfect embodiment of compassion, does not hold an image or idea. There is no prejudice and no filter, no mental activity that leans toward one type of action or another.     Only let it go and follow the method of practice. The compassion that takes care of itself is true Compassion. It is not "made" with your practice.
