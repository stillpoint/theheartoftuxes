    Just lonely... wondering if anyone has any advice

    I don't think arranging the outside forms of your life will get at the root of this, although outside changes may help in other ways.

    When I hear that you are lonely and that you are questioning what you are doing with your life, I am happy. I think that's wonderful. You are coming close to yourself again after being away. It just maybe feels like shit; but inside of that, there's a wonderful unknown, a wonderful new possibility.

    I don't think that feeling lonely is bad. Actually, if you pay attention to the feeling rather than the repeating thought "poor me, I'm alone", there is tremendous poignancy and wisdom there.

    In some sense, we are always alone, even among others; and in some sense we are always together with everything, even on our own. It is not the fact of being isolated that is the problem. The thinking and grasping is the problem. Thinking that things need to be a certain way, and reaching out to get something or manipulate something, puts you in opposition to your life — there's you, and there's the thing you think you're missing. Originally, you are not in opposition, but your thinking and grasping make it so. And this is very uncomfortable; it cuts off your original peace and completion.

    I've been on both sides of this, at times very lonely and agitated, seeking and seeking, grasping and grasping to find a "lifeline" that I thought I needed. And at other times, in sync with myself and inspired to further the things unfolding through me, I couldn't wait to be on my own — space and time away from others became so precious!

    I recommend releasing engagement with outside things; i.e., don't worry about arranging your life to get your best situation; but instead become more intimate with yourself and your inner movements. Where is your interest moving? If you gave yourself the permission, where would your feet and heart move you?

    Life is already unfolding through you. If you become sensitive and responsive to your own inner unfolding (your interest and inspiration), then everything will flow from there, because that is Life's unfolding through you. This depends on listening, not on finding a good idea and imposing it. So sincerity, and open inquiry, and especially the willingness to feel will be very important for you. If you avoid the feeling that goes with loneliness you will be avoiding the place where you, in your solitary vulnerability, are intimate with the ten thousand things.

    Self-permission should be total.

    Also, following your own flow is momentary. It is not something that you achieve heroically in one fell swoop, coming up with the master plan for the next decade or next lifetime. It appears by listening openly in the moment, and being sincere in your momentary activity. If this, right now, is not what you really want to be doing, then drop it and move on to the next thing. In this way, the inner stream of your unfolding becomes a stronger and stronger flow. You apply and re-apply your inner truth rather than forcing yourself into situations based on old templates or imposed ideas.

    Let it come from the inside rather than trying to manipulate outside conditions.

    Said another way, let your individual magnificence be so great, have so much flow, that there is no room for doubt or self-checking.

    .

    As to those other, outside forms that can help: depression can be seen as an unwillingness to move and feel. Physical movement and getting out in the elements, out in Nature, can be very helpful and can kickstart your process of connecting to yourself. At first, your inner flow is covered by stuck, stagnant patterns; but once you get the body and breath moving, other things start to flow as well, because body, breath, and mind all condition each other.

    Exercise, play, and especially free dance are great for this purpose. Other things that can help kickstart the flow: free-form writing, unplanned artwork, singing. Just do it, and keep dropping the thoughts about "I am doing this" or "this is my situation". Just do it, in the moment.
