    Street Zazen

    Who says monks are setting themselves apart from the rest of humanity? Who says that certain clothing is dressing up while other clothing is not?

    The flower unfolding is all material. Without the material the flower is 'draped in', there is no flower and no unfolding. This world of relationships is all a dress-up game.

    The real unity of robe and vow is not what you think. It doesn't come from the level of thought that most of us live in. But without genuine experience of the interpenetration of form and emptiness, we may think that these things all can be grasped merely with ideas, or that they can be touched by arguing 'for' and 'against'.
