    TheHeartofTuxes

    Consider how it is in your life: you are one thing, then you are another, then another. Your 5-year-old self has become something else by now, based on cause, condition, and effect. This is re-becoming (which is a bit different than the idea of a soul taking another body in reincarnation).

    Consider how it is in your life: when you are in a very difficult situation, stretched thin, overwhelmed, at a loss, how easy is it for you to be peaceful and centered, and to have control over your destiny? This shows the movement from higher to lower 're-becoming'.

    When you have gathered positive conditions — a certain amount of wealth and leisure, supportive living situation, a certain level of health and fitness, supportive friends and living partners, good teaching, good and healthy habits, and so on — then you can feel like you can actually make some positive transformations, some headway along the path. Even at that, it is usually a difficult and precarious situation, which can easily be toppled by crisis.

    So how well equipped will you be without those supportive conditions as you head toward hell? How will it be when you have fallen into the grip of greed, frustration, rage, grief... when you have lost your good living space and friends and teachers... and when you are completely surrounded by others utterly driven by their own wild self-centered drama? In one of the genocide villages in Sudan, say....

    Next, subtract the faculty of human intelligence, and subtract the grounding influence of the wakeful physical body. Picture this genocide happening all around you, but in your dream. How much control will you have over your actions? How will you guide yourself toward positive rebirth rather than merely reacting... reacting... reacting... and co-creating the horror?

    This is how Samsara becomes endless. When you go down, you can be stuck down there for a long, long, long time. Just consider how difficult it is for a homeless person to get back on their feet and into positive circumstances. All the issues to be faced: exposure to the elements, financial disempowerment, health issues, fear and other mental instability, shame, self-defeat, exposure to violence, and so on. When you go down, there is no guarantee that you will be able to find your way back up. It often happens only through long and painful purification, and you only return once the results of negative karma that brought you down, and the negative karma you've made in reactivity to your difficult situation, are fully and painfully experienced.

    So yes, you could come back as human, and likely will, countless times among countless other rebirths. But if you go to hell it will be extremely difficult to find your way out, and it will be an extremely long time before you can achieve anything like a positive human birth again, in which you can make progress along the spiritual path.
