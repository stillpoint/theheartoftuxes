    Get it after you've mastered mindfulness. A tattoo will bestow no special power; but it will encourage you to show off and think of yourself as special.

    You want something to bring you back. That's good. So you need a practice. Find a teacher, join a group, and learn a practice. Commit to a practice schedule. Take these supportive steps before pushing yourself to be mindful beyond your capacity. If you push yourself, you will soon rebel against yourself.

    Teacher and practice group are tremendous supports. These are far more grounding than anything you can own or wear.

    If you have a practice that you understand well, then you can make use of external tools. But if you have tools without a practice, you won't make use of them. They will make use of you, by getting you caught up in the wrong things, in the outer form.

    Also, be very careful with this attitude of not letting emotions take over. Emotions are meant to flow. If they don't flow, sickness ensues. And if you try to suppress them, they take up residence in your unconsciousness, controlling you from the shadows.

    Although it seems reasonable and sincere to try to eliminate what you see as the negative parts of yourself, only trying to destroy or avoid these parts makes them into your demons. This requires teaching and a practice environment.

    So much better than any material possession is to possess a non-grasping practice. And much better than any adornment is to be adorned by even-mindedness, not pushing anything away.
