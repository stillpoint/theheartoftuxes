    I could really use some help

    This is a very wonderful post. How fortunate you are!

    How fortunate you are to clearly see the patterns that cause you unhappiness and the general path that can change them. Not everyone can do that.

    How fortunate you are to be willing to follow up on what see, to take steps, to make changes. That is not common.

    How fortunate you are to actually be taking steps -- to be seeking a teacher, trying to make it work by seeing what you can offer in exchange, coming here for support. That is less common still.

    Furthermore, you already have many super powers: Can you see colors and forms? How is that possible? Can you hear sounds? How is that possible? Can you feel sensations and emotions? Can you express yourself and let others know you? Do you have the capacity to listen to others, understand them, and care for them?

    Ultimately, you have the possibility of transformation and awakening. You can move beyond suffering in particular circumstances, and also in an absolute sense, finishing the whole issue.

    So you have many, many miraculous super powers. You are a shapeshifter, a wizard who can take on new roles, new attitudes, new ways of relating. Very miraculous. It's just that you have lived with your miraculous powers every day, and have come to think of them as common and insufficient. Your mind is not “enough", so what you have is not “enough". If you make your mind enough, everything you have or don't have will also be enough.

    There are three levels of good motivation. The lower level of good motivation is to help your life become better so that things will be easier and more beautiful, and you will suffer less. The middle level is to attain enlightenment, perceive your own essential nature, and finish with suffering once and for all.

    Can you guess what the high level of good motivation is? It often doesn't occur to even sincere seekers.

    The high level of motivation is to attain the enlightenment of all beings -- for all beings everywhere to wake up, be free of suffering, and live in correct relationship.

    I feel you are at a stage where you can start to open to the highest motivation. Although it means placing the focus on others rather than yourself, it will help you tremendously; and it will be an important basis for you to find the conditions for your progress (teacher/guide, community, teaching, consistent practice).

    When you have sincere aspiration and generous compassion, your mind is no longer narrow. That allows all manner of enlightened beings and positive influences to support you. Your own broad mind and open heart provide the room for greatness to enter and do its work.

    So in seeking your good situation, don't fall into the trap of narrow-mindedness and selfishness. The more you think about yourself, the more suffering and difficulty you have. Instead, you can have joy about the fact that you are on the path of greatness, the path that leads beyond your small self. No matter where you are on the path, what matters is that you are on the path and it is a wonderful, precious life. Every challenge is part of the path. Every difficulty trains you in moving beyond small-mindedness. Every situation already has the nature of awakening.

    Please take this in. We can talk more about specifics later, and I will try to help you find a teacher in your area. But if you keep your sincere and wide mind and heart, Bodhisattvas will already rush forth to help you.
