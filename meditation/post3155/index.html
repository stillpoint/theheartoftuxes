    TheHeartofTuxes

    The best is a living teacher (accomplished and certified by other teachers) in a proven traditional path. There are many reasons for this, many reasons why a living teacher is most beneficial and why a traditional path is most reliable.

    Sometimes people will say "Oh, but I can't have a teacher. I don't know where to go... It's too far... It's too difficult... I don't have the time... I don't have the money...." and so on. But they say this without having tried!

    So it's a question of what you really want in life, and what you are willing to do to get it. If you look into it, there are many possibilities. If it's difficult, there are ways to make it easier. And difficulty shouldn't be the deciding factor anyway, because difficulty is often correct! (If we only ever do what's easy, we will never attain anything of significant depth.)

    If it's far, you can go less frequently. If you don't have money, you can get work, save up, or look into work-study exchanges or other assistance. If you are very isolated, you can look into study from a distance, via written communication, online interviews, podcasts, meetups, and so on.

    There are many options. But a human teacher who can hold the clear view for you, and make sure you get the right teaching in the right order at the right time, is extremely valuable.

    Sometimes as a start, people can work from a book. But the more ideas people get, the farther off they tend to fall, and the more confused their way becomes. If you do start from a book, I suggest bringing focus to your experiments. Try only one path at a time, and give it enough time to settle in and become a clear experience, a clear experiment, not colored by checking and comparing and mixing other methods. Dig one hole deeply (rather than a bunch of shallow ones), then you can get to treasure.

    Many people here recommend Culadasa's The Mind Illuminated as a very well organized manual of training. Usually if you do some sincere practice and get a taste of stillness you will want to do more. At that time, use the energy and enthusiasm to seek out a proper living teacher.
