    Struggling... Should I try something else first?

    Wonderful! And when you drift away in thought, when you notice it do you return to the method? That is correct practice.

    Only don't check how long you go away for or what the feeling or experience is like. Don't check, only do it. When you get led away, return. If you get led away for a few seconds, return. If you get led away for some hours or weeks, return. Your only concern is returning; don't make judgments and stories about your experience: that's just making more distraction.

    And if you attach to your feeling, that is making more problem. Let your mind be smooth. Repeatedly let go of everything but the method.

    Your going away in thought is a habit created by your past use of your body, speech, and especially your mind. So it has appeared naturally from causes. It is quite reasonable for the habit to be there. And even if you didn't have a habit of attaching to thought, your thinking would continue to jump here and there creating connections and images -- that is the proper function of thinking. Your job is to let go of attachment and return to reality, which means returning to the method.

    Right now at some level you trust your thinking more than you trust your practice. That is why you trade the method and the real moment for going away in thought. This is a habit that has developed over a long time, with supporting conditions. The things that have happened to you, the environments and people around you -- many of these may contribute to your making of the habit.

    But if you made it, you can unmake it. Just don't expect that the habit will immediately disappear. It will disappear when the effort you've put into unmaking (opening your hands and returning to the method) is as big and continuous as the habit energy. If you attach to thinking for 18 hours of the day, don't expect to force silence to appear in a couple of minutes. Just attend to your current activity. In the meantime, don't check. You go away; that is natural. Just come back. That is all. That is good practice.

    When you come back to the method, at that time you are present. That is exceptionally wonderful. It is your human birthright to wake up and live in reality. So to be able to do it for even a moment at a time is very wonderful. For that moment, you are a true human, an aware and real being.

    Don't check, only do it, okay?
