    The thinking about it definitely is not meditation.

    The feeling is just a feeling; it doesn't matter what it indicates. If it indicates some kind of meditative state, you still have to let go of thinking about it and return to open awareness in the moment. If it indicates some self-generated experience or dream state or random firing of nerves or psychological blip, you still have to let it go and return to open awareness in the moment.

    It doesn't matter what anyone else goes through. This question "does anyone else...?" is so common on these forums, and so insidious! It doesn't matter what others go through. As if discovering that 58% of people experiencing something similar would make it somehow significant.... You don't need to dally with what comes and goes in others' minds. You don't even need to dally with what comes and goes in your mind! Don't make drama about the various things that come up for you, much less invite more complication with stories and speculations about things that come up for others.

    Sometimes you nod off, you go into dreamland for a split-second or a minute or ten, and then you jolt back to waking awareness. This can feel like a skip. What matters at this point is returning to your method, not thinking of yourself as the hero of your process. The ego wants you to make something special of everything that happens to you, so that you will stay in the ego's world, in the world of objects. It keeps you thinking of yourself as a self that is experiencing things, so the ego or self-image gets what it wants, which is to survive and to lead the agenda.

    But when you sit in open awareness, the sense of self begins to wane and merge with reality, and the ego becomes desperate to survive. So it sends you thoughts encouraging you to think of everything as so significant you have to hold it and check it and consider it. It will send comfort to lull you; it will send discomfort to generate thoughts of how much you are suffering; and if those fail to grab you it will send unusual sensations and experiences to make you think something amazingly significant or terribly dreadful is happening. What these all have in common is that they regenerate the strong sense of a self going through it all. Happy ego! Poor meditation.

    Don't check. Only follow the method of meditation suited for your ultimate aim. Only do it.
