    is it easier for those who meditate to detect deception/lies?

    Correct meditation brings you closer to reality. Ultimately, correct meditation is reality.

    There are several factors that contribute to enhanced perception: calming of distraction, the ability to focus the attention, seeing through and shedding one's own inner narratives, stabilizing the mind (learning how not to 'take the bait'), developing the intuition and learning the difference between intuition and reactivity.

    As one progresses in meditation (but not just any meditation), these factors emerge as a unification with reality. One's original unity with the world-as-it-is becomes revealed. We may talk in terms of 'becoming one with everything' but that's just an expedient lie: originally we are already everything. As one approach one's original unity, many siddhis (spiritual powers) may appear. One may find it as easy to perceive someone else's energy and experience as it is to perceive one's own, since there is only one body, one mind, anywhere.

    I think almost all people who do proper meditation with any consistency will experience some form of greater insight and psychic power. It is not unusual. But problems arise when one's old attachments have not been purged in a parallel cultivation of character. When one gains new power but maintains the old grasping habits, egoic responses and inner narratives can flare up again. One can think of oneself as special, wiser, 'someone who knows'. It is a very strong draw: on the verge of surrender the ego is vulnerable, so of course it jumps at the chance to again establish itself as a special and powerful being (even if it is a fabrication of the mind).

    So having greater powers of perception is fine; but if you think of yourself as having them, that is already an obscuration, already a departure from open presence; and often enough it becomes the fatal flaw, the blind spot that causes you to think you know something that you don't. Sometimes those 'intuitions' are just your own reactions and psychological material bubbling up from the depths.
