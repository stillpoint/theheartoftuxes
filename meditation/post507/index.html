    Moving Meditation?

    5Rhythms is a great practice, and broader in both training and application than most people realize at first encounter.

    You will have different experiences with different teachers, so be aware that there's more to it than just one event or one perspective.

    There is at least one 5R teacher in Norway, and a few people training to be teachers; and there are several teachers in Sweden, and very many in Europe. Whether there are events in your home town or not, you should be able to find workshops within reach. You can also invite teachers to your city.

    Some 5Rhythms teachers are listed on the 5Rhythms Global website. But there are also people who are certified 5Rhythms teachers, but who aren't members the teacher's association (and thus not listed on the website); so you can also contact the head office in New York to find out about others who may not be listed.

    Hege Gabrielsen

    Some other modalities that have branched out from, or in parallel to, 5Rhythms include Movement Medicine (Ya'acov & Susannah Darling-Khan), Open Floor (Andrea Juhan), and Soul Motion (Vin Marti). All of these teachers have studied with 5Rhythms originator Gabrielle Roth. I've danced with all of them, and can attest to their expertise and integrity.

    Gabrielle Roth passed away in 2012. Among other gifts, she created several CDs with percussion-based renditions of the Rhythms, as well as a couple of books and a couple of videos. You should also be able to find some of her interviews on YouTube and podcast sources.

    Best wishes, and be in touch if I can be of any more assistance.
