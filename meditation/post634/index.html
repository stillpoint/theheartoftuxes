    Well, not awareness. Concentration, or attention. Awareness is never built up, and certainly not built up by limiting focus to a single object.

    Not all forms of meditation are concentration style, nor do all forms involve an object. In fact, in the strict sense of the word, meditation isn't intense focusing at all; there is no duality to it (no subject focusing on an object or withdrawing from other objects).

    But if we are clear on terminology, there's the next point....

    We can agree that concentration is developed through the practice of focusing on a single object, and that there are many possible objects; except that in this case there is following the dot, meaning it moves or changes. What are the movements or changes based on? One may actually not be focusing in this practice, but latching onto some unsettled aspect of self, whether it's mental, physiological, energetic, or mystical.

    I don't know this practice, so I'm asking. But alarm bells go off when I hear about following something vague and changing, because that's reminiscent of the ways some adepts steal the energy of unsuspecting students or otherwise possess them.

    I've met a couple of people who had been beset by such intentional invasions/possessions, both of whom described the experience as hellish. One of them fortunately took up Zen practice as the only effective defense he could find, and later became a great teacher. The other spiralled into despair and mental illness. I've also heard reports of similar possessions from other practitioners, and one of the common threads is that practices of synchronizing movements were taught as a meditation but were actually the vehicle by which the teacher attuned to the student's energy and began to steal it.

    Again, I don't know what is the case here, but vague or self-invented methods ring alarm bells, methods that attune to shifting objects where the cause of the shifting is unclear ring alarm bells, and teachers not affirmed in a tradition and lineage ring alarm bells; so this situation is so far suspicious on all three fronts.
