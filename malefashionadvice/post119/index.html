    Need to pick one pair of shoes to buy for a gray suit.

    Brown shoes are not only the more elegant choice with a grey suit, they will be more versatile in terms of matching with other colors. Black shoes can look good with a grey suit, but create a colorless background palette (to which color can be added in shirt, tie, socks, and pocket square). Black is inherently more conservative; it lacks the character of color, and offers intensity of darkness.

    It seems that many people, especially in North America, who are not well versed in color opt automatically for the sharpness of black. As a basic shoe/leather alternative, brown tends to be considered the more sophisticated choice in Europe; though those are very broad generalizations. The fact remains that black is more difficult to match. It will look bad with some blues, horrible with many browns, and often boring with greys. Black will also be a very stark contrast with brighter colors. Brown will be an easier and better match almost across the board.

    Another flexible option that looks great with grey and several other hues is the range of wine/red-brown/oxblood colored shoes.

    The one area where black is king is in the formal realm. With formal dinner jackets, black suits, and even with a charcoal grey in formal settings, there's no question that black is the favored choice for footwear.

    In terms of negotiating winter weather, as long as your shoes are properly treated they'll be able to handle a bit of snow or puddles. I'll assume that the 'winter' you'll encounter will be more along the lines of walking from parking lot to event venue than trudging through drifts and slush for a half hour. If you expect slush and puddles, you can always wear galoshes until you enter the venue. If you need to look good outside as well, then dress boots come into the picture (and, if you like, they can be in the picture anyway).

    If you'll be standing outside in snow and slush, then you may have to compromise with as nice-looking a waterproof dress boot as you can muster.
