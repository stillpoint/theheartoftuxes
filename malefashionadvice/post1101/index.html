    Need help picking some new dress shirts.

    Context, please. You will want to wear these suits where, for what occasion, doing what activity, with which people? What kind of suits? What is your personal style or character?

    Every suit, shirt, pants, and tie, and every combination of them, expresses something unique. Different expressions will be appropriate for different situations. In order to provide guidance, I/we need to know more about the situations you will be dressing for and the other clothes you will be wearing.

    ~

    > Just stick with white, and grab a couple of light blues. Anything else with a suit will look a bit ridiculous.

    White and blue shirts are certainly safe and versatile. They may form the core of a flexible wardrobe; but to say that no other shirt will work with a suit shows ineptitude. A vast range of colors and patterns will not only go well with suits, but can cast you in a better light, set you apart from the mundane crowd, and create a more interesting and attractive appearance.

    ~

    > Consider a deep maroon... ...aim at the opposite part of the shade spectrum and go for dark versions of the standard colors

    Darker colored shirts may occasionally work with a suit (without a tie) for clubbing -- recreational evenings out -- but they are notoriously difficult to match with ties, may not provide proper contrast with the suit, and are often implicated in fashion disasters like the 'mobster' and the 'stockboy' looks.

    Before suggesting any color of shirt, surely we should know the specific colors of the suits being worn, the OP's skin tone, and the context.
