    In desperate need of good shoes

    Either of those are a big improvement on the runners. The hightops are a bit overworked with all the panels, but not bad, and totally fine for high school. I'd just check that the shade of the shoes is not too similar to the khakis that you'll be wearing, or it could look not so great. You want a bit of contrast; so if you'll be wearing dark khaki and dark jeans, those could be good; and if you'll be wearing light khaki you might want a darker or bolder color.

    For overall versatility, a mid- to dark-brown shoe is probably best, as it will go with almost anything you can throw at it (other than black).

    Your award ceremony sound not so much like a semi-formal (which could involve anything from blazer to a suit and tie, and in some people's books a tuxedo), but rather 'dress nicely'. The style of the Vans doesn't go extremely well with a black shirt, but their grey color helps a bit. Like you say, they'll probably fly under the radar.

    Another option you might want to consider, and one that will allow you to upgrade your style a bit more, is the desert boot. You may be able to get a pair for under $100 (granted, that article is a couple of years old). The crepe sole on a classic desert boot also has some resiliency, so it may be okay on your feet along with orthotics. Taupe color (warm grey, like the Guess boots in the article), whether light or dark, would go with khakis and a black shirt, and would be versatile for many future outfits.
