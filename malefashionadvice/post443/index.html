    Opinions on these type of 'mustard' chinos?

    I don't see any mustard colored chinos in the link. Camel/tan is a very common, basic, and versatile color for pants. What's not to approve of, as long as there's some interest in the color or pattern of another item in the outfit, or in the interplay of pieces?

    If the chinos were a more intense deep yellow, that would also be fine, but different. In place of a neutral color like tan, mustard makes pants into a statement piece. Far from limiting you, the addition of colorful pants can make a previously ho-hum wardrobe into a collection that can offer verve and enjoyability.

    If the color is a deep mustard or a muted yellow, it's not even that wild or risky of a choice; it's a rich and earthy color that will add pop to many neutrals, bring enough color into the outfit for monochromatic patterns to be used elsewhere, or create a rich intensity with deep solids (especially a wide range of blues).

    If the color is a brighter yellow, that's different territory yet again.
