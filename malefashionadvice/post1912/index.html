    Looking for a jacket, casual yet business acceptable and came across this. Any advice?

    It may depend on your business; but in the broad sense of business casual, it doesn't belong in the conversation. It's short, bomber style, made of nylon, has a sheen, and includes a sleeve pocket and elastic cuffs -- fine for casual wear, but not for the office.

    Even if you wear it casually, don't pair it with a tie. That conjures up images of stockboys through the ages. I know the windbreaker and tie has been done; but it's an immature look that suggests one hasn't yet graduated to finer clothes, and anyway is not appropriate in a professional context.

    Bad.

    Dorky.

    Worn specifically as outerwear when schmoozing at an event or out on the street, a leather bomber may work with a tie; but this is still not okay for the office. This is getting there, but still something that one would expect you to take off upon entering the workplace.

    Sportjackets, blazers, peacoats, trenchcoats, and leather or tweed car coats or dress coats would all be a safer bet.
