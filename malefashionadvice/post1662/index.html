    Help with watch band colour

    It really depends on what you usually wear and intend to wear with the watch. You'll want your accessories (shoes, belt, glasses) to be in the same ballpark. In general, brown will be much more versatile than black. The range of outfits that a dark green watch with black strap will match is pretty narrow (think standard army issue). You may want to avoid a brown that is too close in shade (darkness) to the green face. Mid-to-light brown leather straps should look good with the watch, and will go with many different outfits, but in particular with autumn colors and earth tones.

    Although many browns would work, a bit of a red hue in the brown should look good. A light tan strap could offer more interesting contrast, yet with a neutral and versatile color.

    The color of steel may limit your choices. The final word is always trying the strap with the watch, and wearing it to see how it looks on you with your wardrobe; so buy from a store with a good return policy.
