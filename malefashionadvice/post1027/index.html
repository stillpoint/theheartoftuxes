    Ok guys, I'm really skinny (5'9,125lbs) will skinny jeans look good or make me look skinnier?

    To learn about fit on a skinny frame, look to the thinner fashion models in pictures from online catalogs and runway shows, as models tend to be lanky anyway. You'll see the outfits that overemphasize their thinness and the ones that use it to advantage.

    Skinny jeans will make you look rail-thin. This is a look that is employed by women for whom the long-limbed appearance can be considered attractive. Skinny jeans are also used intentionally by some guys in gaunt-hip styles; but they in no way de-emphasize their skinny frames.

    The best fit for you will be clothing that skims comfortably over the body, neither loose nor tight. First of all, seeing every detail of a man's musculature through his clothing is crass (and some will wield that crassness as an "F-you" to onlookers or conversely as an alpha-male statement of intent). Secondly, skin-tight clothing will reveal the thinness of thin limbs.

    Overly loose clothing will also make you look skinny, because you will clearly not be filling them out. Not too tight and not too loose is best, and the margin is small for you, so make good friends with your tailor.

    For great examples of proper fit on a skinny frame, study Bob Dylan in the 1960s.
