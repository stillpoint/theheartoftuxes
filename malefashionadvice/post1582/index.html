    Black bowtie with a suit?

    See, there you're adding a shock of color that allows the jaunty side of the bowtie to come through. I don't have any personal experience with this one, but I imagine that with a bright pink shirt, the black bowtie could work; and if we start the experiment with dots, for example, I find this and this fun without being totally wacky. The variation of shades here is interesting; but the size of the dots is getting 'out there'. This tie is fantastic -- it stays classy, but vibrant.

    With a lighter pink, this maroon along with another maroon accent in the cuff links or socks (or shoe laces??) could also fly. Darker purple also works very well. (Which may bring you back to considering a very classy straight grenadine tie).

    Stripes and foulard and other patterns, depending on how bold a statement you want to make.
