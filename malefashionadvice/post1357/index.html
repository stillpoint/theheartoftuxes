    Any know where I can find this tie?

    Search Google for "pindot tie" or "polka dot tie". Polka dots, to use the phrase correctly, are larger and more jubilant; but the term is often used to refer to any dot pattern.

    It's a fairly classic pattern, so it should be offered by many of the online tie stores as well as many brick-and-mortar men's clothing stores. To approximate that particular tie, you'll want to purchase one in a narrow width, under 3". A 2.5" tie is narrow enough to look youthful, but won't paint you into a corner style-wise, and can be less susceptible to shifts in fashion trends than a downright skinny tie (which JGL's pictured tie may be â€” likely around 2").

    If you're set on that look and can't find a skinny pindot, buy a regular width and have it narrowed by a tailor. Not all tailors will undertake the project; some will.
