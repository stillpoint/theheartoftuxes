    Colours that would go with tan jeans?

    Tan is a neutral, so just about anything will "go". But it's up to you to take care of the overall impression of the outfit; and in large part this means controlling contrast.

    If the tan jeans are light shade, a light blue OCBD will look light and airy. There will not be a lot of defining contrast, and both colors will be calm and unassuming. That's fine for some character types and skin colors, and it makes others disappear too much. A complete outfit in muted colors can be very sophisticated; but do you want that much "calm and unassuming"? A light shirt with a dark sweater will add verve to the outfit via contrast. There will literally be a bolder sense of definition, and that will translate to the feel of the outfit.

    With light neutral jeans, a mid-to-dark top part of the outfit should provide good contrast. With dark neutral jeans, a light top will be required. And with medium shades of jeans, you may have to work harder to inject contrast (with very light or dark tops), or your interest may have to come elsewhere, through color or pattern, for instance.

    On my screen those jeans look mid-shade; so you may indeed consider whether your other pieces will look sharply defined with them.
